ABOUT

This is the journey of a 1970 Chevelle Malibu’s conversion from a 250 straight six with a two speed power glide into a V8 4 speed manual car. This project has been roughly 10 years, it has hit tons of speed bumps, and taken way too long. I have dealt with great people, terrible people, been ripped off and learned so much about GM-A body cars.

Feel free to inquire about renting this car for photo shoots/video work, ask about parts/fitting issues or just take a look! The other car became a project when I didn’t have the heart to cut up a 4 door. As they say, a dollar and a dream, because cars.
